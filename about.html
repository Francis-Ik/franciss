About LMC

The LMC event at TCGC School is a highly anticipated annual gathering that celebrates the spirit of competition and excellence among students. This event is designed to showcase the diverse talents of the student body through a variety of competitions, including academic challenges, performance arts, and leadership activities. Students compete in different categories, demonstrating their skills, creativity, and knowledge. The LMC event not only fosters healthy competition but also promotes teamwork, discipline, and personal growth, making it a cornerstone of student life at TCGC School. It is a celebration of the hard work, dedication, and talent of the students, making it one of the most exciting events in the school calendar.
